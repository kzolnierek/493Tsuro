Welcome to Tsuro!



Steps before starting the game:

1) Enter your name

2) Select your player color

3) Hit submit

4) After hitting submit wait for all players to join before continuing to the next step in the game

5) Once all users are present, hit "All Users Present. Let's Play!"

Once you are on the game page:

1) Click where you want your player to start. Your starting space must be one of the tick marks along the perimeter of the board.

2) Click multiple times for him to switch tick marks or click another square to switch completely.

3) When done, wait for time to expire and then let the game begin!

4) Pick tiles from your hand and play them, remembering the goal to stay on a path and not be pushed off the board. Tiles can be roated once placed on the board by clicking on the tile.

5) Once a player is pushed of the edge, both players are notified of the results of the game and the game is ended.

Good luck and have fun!
